      How to Play

      1. The host picks a topic and starts the game.
      2. Every player sees a 4x4 word grid. One word is the secret word.
      3. One player is secretly the Chameleon and does not know the secret word.
      4. Each player gives a one-word clue related to the secret word.
      5. The Chameleon must bluff a convincing clue without knowing the word.
      6. After all clues are given, players vote on who they think the Chameleon is.
      7. If the Chameleon gets the most votes, the players win.
      8. If the Chameleon avoids being caught, the Chameleon wins.
